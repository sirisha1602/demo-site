Power Your Business with Spinnaker As- A- Service


Subscribe to OpsMx SAS Open Enterprise Spinnaker (OES) solution to leverage the continuous delivery platform for releasing software changes faster & with accuracy. 


Buy Now

Open Enterprise Spinnaker (OES) solution is now also available as Software-as- a-Service (SaaS) model. With SaaS OES offering, enterprises can on-board easily & faster on Spinnaker, doesn’t have to pay upfront heavy cost & can scale up at their will. 

Benefits of SAS OES :

Affordable

No need to pay upfront costs, instead you make payment on monthly basis based on no of pipelines & the duration for availing services, making it Operating expenses (OPEX). This model removes the need for annual contracts. It saves the infrastructure, maintenance & support cost.

Worry free IT

No need to worry about the maintenance of Spinnaker or the hardware it resides on, compatibility and upgrades will be taken care by OpsMx.


High levels of security 

Robust security measures are in place beyond the affordability of most businesses safeguarding your confidential data. The data is much safer in the cloud than on a server in your offices



Quick deployment

Spinnaker on-boarding can be done much faster over the Internet compared to on premise applications which needs to be installed on a the physical server.

Scalability

Provides greater flexibility as you only pay for what you use and can easily scale up to meet demand.

Lower Total Cost of Ownership 

 Significantly reduces the energy bills as it does away with on premise servers & ACs required for cooling.
